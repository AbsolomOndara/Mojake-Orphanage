Beyond Shelter, We Build Futures

At Mojake Orphanage, we believe that every child deserves more than just a roof over their head. We provide quality education, emotional support, healthcare, and a loving family environment.

Our holistic approach nurtures the mind, body, and spirit — helping children grow into confident, self-reliant adults who give back to their community.
