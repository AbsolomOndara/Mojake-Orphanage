Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Mojake Orphanage.

How can I sponsor a child?

Visit our Sponsor a Child page, choose a child, and follow the donation process. You can also contact us directly for more information.

Can I visit the orphanage?

Yes, we welcome visitors. Please arrange an appointment by emailing us at least two weeks in advance. We'll be happy to show you around.

What volunteering opportunities are available?

We need teachers, medical volunteers, activity coordinators, and more. Send us a message with your skills and availability, and we'll find a role for you.

How are donations used?

100% of donations go to children's education, healthcare, food, and shelter. We publish annual reports for full transparency.

Can I donate items instead of money?

Yes, we accept donations of clothes, books, toys, and educational materials. Please contact us to coordinate drop-off or shipping.

Do you offer internships?

We offer internships for social work, education, and healthcare students. Contact us with your academic background and interests.
